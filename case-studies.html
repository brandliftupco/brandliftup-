Healthcare · Ayurveda
🌿
VEDICUPCHAR
How we helped India's leading Ayurvedic healthcare brand scale their digital patient acquisition across 1,200+ cities with Google Ads, SEO, and WhatsApp automation.
The Challenge
VedicUpchar, founded by Dr. Anil Bansal, had over 50 years of Ayurvedic expertise and 2M+ patient consultations, but their digital presence wasn't matching their offline reputation. They needed to reach patients across India digitally, drive online consultation bookings, and build trust in their e-commerce product line.
Our Strategy
We built a multi-channel digital strategy — Google Ads targeting health condition keywords ("ayurvedic treatment for diabetes", "hair fall solution ayurveda"), Local SEO across 1200+ city pages, GMB optimization for their Delhi NCR clinic, WhatsApp chatbot for instant patient qualification and appointment booking, and Meta Ads with patient testimonial creatives targeting health-conscious audiences.

Logistics · Pan India Transport
🚛
AUTO PICKUP
How we digitally transformed Auto Pickup Petro Chem Pvt. Ltd.'s online presence — from zero digital footprint to a lead-generating machine across Pan India.
The Challenge
Auto Pickup, a Pan India transport and logistics company specializing in general goods delivery, relied entirely on offline referrals. They had no website, no social media, and no digital advertising. Business owners couldn't find them online, and they were losing customers to digitally-savvy competitors.
Our Strategy
Built a professional website highlighting their services — 30-minute lorry receipt, 4-5 day Pan India delivery, door-to-door service, zero damage guarantee, and live tracking. Ran targeted Google Ads for logistics keywords ("transport company Delhi", "goods delivery service"), optimized GMB for local searches, created social media presence showcasing their fleet and delivery success stories, and set up WhatsApp for instant quotation requests.
